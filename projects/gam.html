GAM . Galleria d'arte moderna Identity

Galleria d’Arte Moderna is a modern museum presented in a neon-classistic building. For the logo, we took inspiration from the interior decoration, like the flowers and the repetion of the shapes. But to represent the modern art, this identity is created with two main bright colors.

he shape in the logo is used in front of the Images, to make sure it doesn’t show the full art yet. Also used as a window in the ticket design to show as binoculars where the client is going to.
