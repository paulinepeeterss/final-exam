MM . Mind Matter Magazine

A printed and online magazine to help parents and their children, who struggle with mental health issues. Each month there will be a different issue, these are seperated by different use of colors.

In the magazine there are articles about the issue itself, where the reader can learn more about the symptoms and get tips on how to act around their child with a mental health issue. The children can also learn more about it while playing the games of the magazine. In the end there are stories about children and/or parents who struggle with the same issues.
